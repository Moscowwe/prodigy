Our Programs

Tailored Paths
for Every Stage

Age-appropriate programs designed by child development experts, where curiosity is celebrated and every milestone matters.

01

Baby Class 1

2 – 2.5 years

A gentle and nurturing introduction to early learning. At this stage, children begin to explore separation, build trust, and develop early sensory and motor skills.

02

Baby Class 2

2.5 – 3 years

Children become more independent, expressive, and socially aware. We support early language development, social interaction, and self-expression through creative play.

03

Pre KG

3 – 4 years

Growing curiosity and readiness for more structured learning. We focus on cognitive development, early literacy, and teamwork through hands-on experiences.

04

KG 1

4 – 5 years

School readiness with stronger independence and critical thinking. We enhance academic readiness and problem-solving through a balanced blend of Montessori learning.

05

KG 2

5 – 6 years

Advanced preparation for primary school with a focus on reading, writing, math foundations, and social-emotional skills that ensure a confident transition.
